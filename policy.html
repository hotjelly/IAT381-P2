Delivery

Delivery is available in most areas across Canada. Your order can be delivered by Parcel. A third party vendor has been contracted to perform your delivery service. To ensure that your delivery is successful, your participation is needed.
Hat Corner relies on the information you provide when placing your order. Providing us with accurate, current and complete information will ensure that your delivery is successful and that no additional fees or delays will apply. If you wish to make an address change after your order has been paid for, please contact Hat Corner. We will be happy to assist you at: 1-604-HAT(428)-0001

Estimated Delivery Date

Please note that this is not a guaranteed delivery date for your order. The estimated delivery date reflects when the delivery provider receives your order.

Parcel Delivery

FedEx delivers between 8am to 5pm. Three delivery attempts will be made for Parcel deliveries. At each attempt, you will be left a notice of delivery by FedEx. After the 3rd attempt, the package is returned to the FedEx warehouse nearest to you, where it will be held for 5 days for you to pick up. If not picked up, your parcel order will be returned to Hat Corner. We will issue a credit for your merchandise only. The delivery fee is non-refundable.

Return Policy

When you order or purchase an Hat Corner product including through our website, you may be required to give us contact information, including your home address, phone number and email address, and payment information (such as credit card information) so we may process your order and for billing and delivery, customer service and warranty purposes. When you pay for an Hat Corner product using a credit card, we use your credit card information only to fulfill your order and for other internal purposes.

Cancelling Orders

If you wish to cancel an order that has already left the warehouse, please note that shipping fees still apply. Hat Corner will not assume transport charges for any items returned, unless specifically agreed upon beforehand for products purchased. Please contact Hat Corner at 1-604-HAT(428)-0001 to cancel your Home Shopping order.
