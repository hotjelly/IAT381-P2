Product Story

Story Title

This Dragon Pro was made in Jun 02,2013. I, Kristen William was the lead designer to this hat. It was actually inspired by the Chinese dragon boat festival in the middle of June. I was trying to integrate the idea the of action of the dragon, color of summer and passionate feeling of people in the event. There was several iterations before this final product was launched. The style is mixed with modern and vintage feel which the backdrop was a bit dull but it still has many colourful elements mixing around. The material Ii applied was fabric.There is only one size for this product.
